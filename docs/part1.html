  3. Let ci be the i-th week search interest of a given search term. Calculate the (k + 1)-week averages, defined by
    $$ \hat c_i^k = \frac 1 {k + 1} \sum_{l = i - k}^{i} c_l\,,$$
    and the derivation
    $$ \delta_i^k = c_i - \hat c_i^k\,.$$
    Plot the 4- and 8-week averages and their deviations. What trends do you observe?
  4. Create a Markdown document containing the plots and your observations.
  5. Using the csv package, write your calculated averages and the deviations to a file.
